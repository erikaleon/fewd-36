These cookies are...

  • Perfectly thin
  • Rich and buttery
  • Crispy on the edges
  • Balanced with a hint of sea salt

To make these, you'll need:

  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 tsp water
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups of dark chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350° F
  2. Grease 2 cookie sheets
  3. In a large bowl, stir together flour, baking soda, and salt
  4. In another bowl, mix the butter, sugars, water, and vanilla until smooth
  5. Add eggs to the mixture and mix them lightly
  6. Stir in the flour mixture, then fold in the chocolate chips
  7. Place 2 tbsp of the cookie dough onto the greased cookie sheets 2 inches apart
  8. Bake for 15 minutes or until the edges are slightly burnt. Mmm... Carbon
  9. Remove from oven and enjoy warm with a glass of cold milk
